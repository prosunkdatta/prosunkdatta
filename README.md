# 💫 About Me:
I am Prosun Datta (legally, Prosun Kanti Datta) — a Physics-Informed Machine Learning (PIML) researcher and computational programmer.<br><br>Physics doesn't negotiate with data. My work is built on that premise — encoding physical laws, symmetries, and constraints directly into neural architectures so that models remain thermodynamically consistent, interpretable, and reliable even under distribution shifts, data scarcity, and extreme conditions.<br><br>My technical focus spans physics-constrained loss functions, hybrid PIML architectures, PDE-informed regularization, and rigorous benchmarking against deterministic physical baselines. I am drawn to problems where data-driven approaches alone break down — where physical structure is not optional, but necessary.<br><br>I founded Dynexly — Dynamic Neural Exploration Lab — as the institutional home for open, rigorous PIML research oriented toward climate-vulnerable regions and the infrastructure challenges of the Global South.<br><br>The next frontier in applied ML is not scale — it is physical fidelity. I intend to spend the next decade working toward that.
